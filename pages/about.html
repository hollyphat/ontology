Research Motivation

Today, People visit their doctor in hospital for one or other ailment not knowing that a lot of traditional herb available at their door step which is more affordable, available and cost effective. Subsequently, the need arose to develop an herbal repository containing the taxonomy hierarchy that represents the structured knowledge of herbs and could be integrated in many medical applications that use the herbs in the treatment in an accurate and effective way.

Aims and Objectives

The aim of this work is to develop a mobile ontology system for local herbs in Yoruba land. Objectives of the system includes:

  1. To make available details information about some local herbs including their botanical and local name through the use of android mobile phones
  2. To develop a system that provide users step by step guide on how to use traditional herb to cure some diseases.
  3. To develop a system that provides real-time and immediate information about traditional medicine through the use of local herbs.
